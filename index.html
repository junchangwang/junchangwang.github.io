About Me

Junchang Wang is a researcher at Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (NJUPT). He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). Prior to joining NJUPT, he was a visiting student at Yale University, visiting scholar at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), and staff engineer at Lenovo as well as startups.

His research focuses on parallel and distributed computing, with a particular emphasis on its applications in database and network systems.
