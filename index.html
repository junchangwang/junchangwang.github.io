About Me

Junchang Wang is a lecturer at Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (NUPT). He received his Ph.D. degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in computer science. Before join NUPT, he was a visiting student at Yale University, visiting scholar at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, and staff engineer in Lenovo and startups.

His research focuses on parallel and distributed computing.
